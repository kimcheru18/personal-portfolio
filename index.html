About Me

Hello, I am Kimberly,


Deciding to pursue a career in web development was a scary but exciting decision. It all started when I was asked to collaborate on a company project.


Pairing creativity and functionality, I collaborated with the IT team to construct a scheduling program. Working alongside the web development expert in the company, I saw our ideas come to life. After that, how could I go back to the humdrum realities of administrative work? And so I set out on this journey to discover the possibilities of coding.


I am now a full-stack web developer and graduate of the Fullstack Web Development Program at Thinkful. I am looking forward to being part of a diverse team that I can learn from and contribute to.


I am excited to hear from you!

BEK Lingo

This is a simple communication app for someone suffering from ataxia or other language impairment. There are other apps for this condition but they typically require that the user know what words they are looking for in order to create a sentence.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, NodeJS, React, MongoDB, Mongoose, Express, Mocha, Chai, mLab, Heroku

Statement of Purpose

This app helps one to create a Statement of Purpose. Writing this statement is an assignment to discover and create a verbal representation of who you are and what is important to you. It is an idea to aspire to, a direction to go.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, NodeJS, React, MongoDB, Mongoose, Express, Mocha, Chai, mLab, Heroku

What's your perfect coffee?

This app includes 20 different coffee drinks from different countries. A person can read through the descriptions, view the images and choose a coffee. An overlay will then show where the coffee can be found, two YouTube videos on how to make it and some coffee trivia from Wikipedia.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery

Women Changing Automotive History

This is a quiz app about women who were leaders and innovators in the automotive industry. Test your knowledge on these amazing women who changed history.
